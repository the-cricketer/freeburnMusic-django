Freeburn Music League Format

Freeburn Music League is a reality music competition that is for independent professional and aspiring musicians. Any artist that is unsigned, or signed for distribution but remains independent is eligible to apply for a spot to compete in this 40 week league contest.

The top 500 successful applicants will then compete for a qualification spot in the qualifiers to be in the top 40 of their specific genre/category.

The remaining 300 will be bubbling under with a chance to eliminate a fellow competitors for a spot in the top 40 out of the 100 eligible players in each category.

The league has 5 categories. Applicants must know their music and understand what genre and subgenre their music style/ability falls under.

To win the contestants must then accumulate points throughout the season via plays , fan following, weekly challenges, votes, hits and downloads. Their weekly total score times the algorithm formula will determine the total points and their ranking on the Billboard chart.

With season progress, some top artists will qualify to compete on Freeburn Live, which scores more points from weekly live performance competition, whilst all top 40 artists will be part of the Freeburn Music Reality Show throughout the season which will be streamed weekly.

Artists must compete as a team. Meaning each team must compose of 3-5 members including the main applicant. Team members will assist with roles related to PR, Styling, Management of Competition Tasks/duties and Personal Assistance. This is advisable but not an obligation.

To increase your chances of winning the Billboard Championship or the Genre Championship, artists must fight to be at the top of their genre or the top 100.

If eliminated from either the premium live contest or from the top 40. Acts must fight through the various ways of gathering points to get back into the top 40 or premier League.
